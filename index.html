About Me

Kristen West is experienced in leading and conducting program evaluation and research projects with social service organizations. She has worked on many multi-year projects with state agencies In Utah such as the Division of Child and Family Services, the Department of Health, the Division of Substance Abuse and Mental health, and the Division of Juvenile Justice Services. Her areas of focus include research design in the social sciences, implementation science, and designing training and support materials to help organizations incorporate evidence-based recommendations. She has expertise in data processing and analysis of administrative and other types of data collected for public agencies.
